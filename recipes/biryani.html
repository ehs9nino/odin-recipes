Biryani

How to make this traditional Pakistani Biryani

Pakistani biryani is heavy on spice (and flavor!), and is often made with biryani masala, or simply put, a medley of spices that come together to give it a distinct, masaledar (or spice-laden) taste.

Ingredients to make chicken biryani

  • 1 tsp roasted cumin seeds or black cumin seeds (shahzeera)
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns (kaali mirch)
  • 1/4 tsp fennel (saunf)
  • 1/4 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)

Steps to make chicken biryani

  1. Fry onions in oil for 10-12 minutes on medium heat & make sure they are dry brown
  2. Add garlic ginger and mix for 2-3 minutes on low heat
  3. Now add chicken meat and mix for 3-4 minutes on high heat and add biryani masala after that and a little amount of water sparkle
  4. Add four tomatoes and fry 4-5 minutes on low heat & fry until tomatoes are soft
  5. Add one cup curd or yougurt and mix it after add some corriander, mint and cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat
  6. Now cover and cook for 20 minutes and stir it in between
  7. Boil the rice separatlely until they're 90% ready
  8. Put half of rice in one Karahi & then put the chicken in it and then again put a layer of rice on it
  9. Cook on low heat for ten minutes & after mix it, the Biryani is ready
