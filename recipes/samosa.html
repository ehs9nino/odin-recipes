Samosa "Best Pakistani Snack" recipe

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, cheese, or lentils. It may take different forms, including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region. The word samosa can be traced to the Persian word sanbosag. The pastry's name in other countries can also derive from this root, such as the crescent-shaped sanbusak or sanbusaj in the Arab world, sambosa in Afghanistan, shingara in Bengal, samosa in Pakistan, samosa in India, (Samboso/sambosa‎), samboosa in Tajikistan, sambosa or samosa in Madagascar, samsa by Turkic-speaking nations, sambusa by the Amharas of Ethiopia, sambuus by Somalis of Somalia, Djibouti, Somali Region of Ethiopia and North Eastern Province of Kenya, and chamuça in Goa (India).

Ingredients of Samosa

  • 500 gm all purpose flour
  • 4 boiled potato
  • salt as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kasoori methi powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala powder

How to make samosa

  1. Step 1 Knead the dough
  2. Step 2 Prepare the potato filling
  3. Step 3 Make roti and cut into a triangular shape
  4. Step 4 Fry the samosas
