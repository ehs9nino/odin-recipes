Haleem

Hyderabadi Traditional Haleem Recipe

Lose yourself in Hyderabadi cuisine with this Hyderabadi Haleem Recipe. Try this flavourful mutton haleem recipe today!

Ingredients of Hyderabadi Haleem

  • 2 kilograms mutton
  • 2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 5 tablespoon urad dal
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 cup yoghurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup cashews
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 1/2 cup mint
  • water as required
  • 3 tablespoon yellow moong dal
  • 2 cup broken wheat (dalia)
  • 2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 5 tablespoon chana dal
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 cup onion
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 cup coriander leaves
  • 6 green chilli
  • 3 tablespoon toor daal
  • salt as required

How to make Hyderabadi Haleem

  1. Step 1 Soak broken wheat for 30 minutes
  2. Step 2 Pressure cook mutton for 15-20 minutes
  3. Step 3 Boil broken wheat with all the dals
  4. Step 4 Cook the shredded lamb with whole spice
  5. Step 5 Add blended broken wheat and dal mixture to the pan
